Where to play padel in Antalya

Padel courts in Antalya are concentrated in three areas: Konyaaltı (west, near the coast), Muratpaşa / Lara (city centre and east) and the Belek resort belt. Below are the dedicated clubs we can verify.

How much does it cost?

Prices vary by club, time and season. For reference, 2000 TL / 60 min (≈500 TL pp for four), 3000 TL / 90 min, 4000 TL / 2h at V7 Padel Antalya. For other clubs, ask directly.

Playing without a partner

You don't need to bring three friends. Community clubs run open games and mixers where solo players are matched into a game by level. Message the club through its app or WhatsApp and ask to join an open session.

Who builds the courts

Many courts in Antalya and beyond are built locally. Mediterra Padel is an Antalya-based padel court manufacturer (not a club): they design, produce and install courts for clubs and resorts worldwide.
