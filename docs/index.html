Where to play padel in Antalya

Padel has grown fast in Antalya over the last few years. Courts are concentrated in Konyaaltı (west, near the coast), Muratpaşa / Lara (city centre and east) and the Belek resort belt. Below are the main dedicated clubs we can verify; resort hotels in Belek also offer courts to guests.

Offers coaching for all levels, an active calendar of leagues, tournaments, club games and mixers, plus lounge, locker rooms, showers, parking and equipment rental. Other courts & hotel facilities

Beyond the dedicated clubs above, a number of resort hotels in the Belek belt have padel courts (often guest-priority). For a wider, regularly-updated list of courts across the province, directory sites such as PadelKöy are a good reference. We only list venues above that we can verify as genuine padel clubs open to the public.

Who builds the courts

Many of the padel courts you'll play on in Antalya — and across Turkey and beyond — are built locally. Mediterra Padel is an Antalya-based padel court manufacturer (not a club): they design, produce and install panoramic and classic courts for clubs and resorts worldwide. Worth knowing if you're a club operator or hotel planning to add courts.

Are we missing a real, public padel club, or is information out of date? This guide is updated regularly and lists only verified clubs — corrections welcome.

How much does it cost?

Prices vary by club, time of day and season. As a concrete reference, V7 Padel Antalya charges 2,000 TL for a 60-minute court (about 500 TL per person when four play), 3,000 TL for 90 minutes and 4,000 TL for 2 hours. Coaching (private or group) and membership packages are priced separately. For other clubs, ask directly — rates differ.

Playing without a partner

You don't need to bring three friends. Community clubs run open games and mixers where solo players are matched into a game by level. V7 Padel, for example, organises club games and matchmaking almost daily — message the club through its app or WhatsApp and ask to join an open session.

Coaching & tournaments

Dedicated clubs offer lessons from beginner (grip, court movement, basic strokes) through to advanced training camps with visiting high-level coaches. Tournaments, leagues and club formats run throughout the season — a good way to meet other players and improve quickly.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I play padel in Antalya?

Mainly in Konyaaltı and Muratpaşa/Lara, plus resort hotels in Belek. The main dedicated public clubs are V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı, 4 courts) and Lara Padel Club (Muratpaşa, outdoor).

How much is a court?

It depends on the club. At V7 Padel, a court is 2,000 TL for 60 min (≈500 TL per person for four), 3,000 TL for 90 min, 4,000 TL for 2 hours. Other clubs vary — ask directly.

Can I play without a partner?

Yes — community clubs such as V7 Padel run open games and mixers for solo players. Book through the club's app or WhatsApp.

Is there indoor padel?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya in Konyaaltı has 3 covered, climate-controlled courts (plus 1 outdoor), playable year-round.
