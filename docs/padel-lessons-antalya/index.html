Where can I take padel lessons in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı, Hürriyet Cad. No: 51) offers private and group coaching for all levels, run by qualified coaches. Contact the club via WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) or the Matchpoint app for the current coaching schedule and pricing.

Can a complete beginner take padel lessons in Antalya?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya runs beginner sessions covering grip, footwork, basic strokes and court positioning. Group beginner classes are a cost-effective way to learn; private lessons give faster individual progress. Ask for the intro package when you contact the club.

How much do padel lessons cost in Antalya?

Coaching prices vary by format (private vs group) and duration. Contact V7 Padel Antalya directly for the current rate card — pricing changes seasonally and packages are available for multiple sessions.

Is there padel coaching for children in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya runs junior sessions and camps. Ask the club about the current junior programme when you book — availability depends on season and coach schedule.

Can I book a padel lesson as a tourist on holiday in Antalya?

Yes — one-off private or group lessons are available without membership. Book in advance via WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) to confirm coach availability, especially in peak summer months.

Is there an intensive padel training camp in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya hosts training camps including sessions with visiting coaches. The club's multilingual staff (TR/EN/RU/UA/DE) means camps attract players from across Europe and the CIS. Check with the club for upcoming camp dates.
