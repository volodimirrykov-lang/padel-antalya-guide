How to book a padel court in Antalya

Booking a padel court in Antalya is simple once you know how clubs work: most take bookings through an app or WhatsApp, and community clubs will match you into a game if you come alone.

How do I book a padel court in Antalya?

At V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı) you can book through the Matchpoint app or simply by WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877). The club is open daily 07:00–00:00. Other clubs in the city usually take bookings via Instagram DM or phone.

Can I book a padel court in Antalya without a partner?

Yes. Community clubs run open games and mixers where solo players are matched by level into a foursome. At V7 Padel Antalya, message the club on WhatsApp and ask to join an open game — this is the normal way solo players get on court.

What is the cancellation policy for padel courts in Antalya?

Policies vary by club. At V7 Padel Antalya the cancellation window is 24 hours — cancel earlier than 24h before your slot to avoid losing the booking fee. Always confirm the club's current policy when booking.

Are padel courts in Antalya covered (playable in rain or heat)?

Partly. V7 Padel Antalya has 4 panoramic courts: 3 open-sided roofed courts (a roof for shade from sun and rain, open sides for natural airflow — not a sealed, air-conditioned hall) and 1 open-air, so play continues in rain or summer heat under the roofed courts. Many other courts in the city are fully open-air.

What hours can you play padel in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya is open every day 07:00–00:00, so early-morning and late-night slots are available; evening prime time (about 18:00–22:00) books out fastest. Hotel and municipal courts usually keep shorter hours.
