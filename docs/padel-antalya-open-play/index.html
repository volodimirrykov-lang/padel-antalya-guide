How to play padel in Antalya without a partner — open games and social play

You do not need to arrive with four players to play padel in Antalya. Community clubs run open games and social mixers where solo visitors and locals are matched into games by level.

Can I play padel in Antalya if I don't know anyone?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya runs regular open games where players join as individuals and are matched into groups of four by level. Message the club on WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) and ask to be added to the next open session — staff are multilingual (TR/EN/RU/UA/DE).

What is a padel open game?

An open game (also called a mixer or social) is a structured session where the club organises groups of four from players who sign up individually. You get a full 60–90 minute game without needing to bring your own group. V7 Padel Antalya runs these regularly as part of its community programme.

How do I join an open padel game in Antalya?

Contact V7 Padel Antalya via WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) or the Matchpoint app and ask about the next open game or mixer. The club will confirm timing, level and the court fee (same as regular court hire: 2,000 TL / 60 min split between four players).

Is there a padel community in Antalya I can join?

V7 Padel Antalya has an active local community with a regular tournament calendar, leagues, and social play. The club has strong Russian-speaking, Ukrainian and Turkish player bases, and welcomes players of all nationalities. Ask about joining the club's community groups when you book.

Are there padel tournaments in Antalya for recreational players?

Yes — V7 Padel Antalya runs regular tournaments for all levels, including recreational and beginner formats. Check the club's current tournament schedule via WhatsApp or the Matchpoint app.

What level do I need to be to join an open game in Antalya?

Open games at V7 Padel Antalya cater to all levels from beginner upwards. When you contact the club, mention your experience level so they can place you in an appropriate group.
