Padel in Antalya for visitors — roofed & open-air courts, playing solo, prices

Padel is easy to play in Antalya even as a visitor — with roofed courts that give shade from the sun (they are not air-conditioned), and even if you arrive without a partner.

← Antalya padel guide
Are there roofed padel courts in Antalya?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya in Konyaaltı has 3 roofed courts (plus 1 open-air) that give shade from the sun and cover from rain. The courts are not air-conditioned, so it stays warm under the roof in midsummer.

Can I play padel in Antalya without a partner?

Yes. Community clubs such as V7 Padel run open games and mixers where solo players are matched into a game by level. Message the club through its app or WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) and ask to join an open session.

How much does a padel court cost in Antalya?

At V7 Padel Antalya a court is 2,000 TL for 60 minutes (about 500 TL per person for four players), 3,000 TL for 90 minutes and 4,000 TL for 2 hours. Coaching and memberships are priced separately. Other clubs vary — ask directly.

Where can I take padel lessons in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya offers private and group coaching for all levels, from beginners (grip, movement, basic strokes) to advanced training camps with visiting coaches. Ask the club for current coaching packages.
