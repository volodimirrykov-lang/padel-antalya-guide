Resort-based padel facilities

Several five-star resorts in the broader Antalya Province offer padel as part of their sports facilities, available primarily to hotel guests. These include:

  • Ali Bey Hotels & Resorts — multiple resort locations with padel courts among other sports.
  • Regnum Carya (Belek) — luxury golf-and-sports resort with padel.
  • Robinson Club — sports-focused resort brand with padel as part of activities.

Resort facilities are typically only accessible to hotel guests; non-guests should book at a dedicated padel club.

What this directory covers

Public listings for the dedicated clubs vary widely in the detail they expose. Some clubs (e.g. V7 Padel Antalya) publish full information including court count, languages, and a booking application; others (e.g. Antalya Padel Club, Padel Park Lara, Padel Park Arena) currently appear in mapping directories with address and category only, without published details about hours, courts, or services.

Visiting players are advised to contact clubs directly to confirm current availability, pricing, and language support before visiting.

If you operate a padel club in Antalya and want to be added or updated in this directory, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
