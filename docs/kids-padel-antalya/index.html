Where can kids learn padel in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı) runs a padel school with group and individual training for children, daily 07:00–00:00. Contact the club by WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) to ask about kids' groups and a first lesson.

How much do kids' padel lessons cost in Antalya?

At V7 Padel Antalya (2026 price list): group kids' lesson from 800 TL with a club coach (8-lesson pack 6,000 TL); individual kids' lesson from 2,600 TL (club coach) or 3,400 TL (head coach), with packs of 3/5/8/10 lessons cheaper per lesson. Prices change seasonally — confirm when booking.

What age can children start playing padel?

Most clubs take children from primary-school age; the smaller court and slower ball make padel easier to pick up than tennis. Ask the club to place your child in a group by age and level — a trial lesson is the usual way to start.

Is padel safe for children?

Padel is a low-impact sport: underarm serve, no heavy overhead smashes at beginner level, and the enclosed court keeps the ball in play. Kids play with coaches in structured groups, which keeps the game controlled and fun.
