Padel court prices in Antalya — what to expect in 2026

Court prices in Antalya are charged per court per hour, split between up to four players — so padel is often cheaper per person than tennis. Here is what verified clubs currently charge.

How much does a padel court cost in Antalya?

At V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı) a court costs 2,000 TL for 60 minutes, 3,000 TL for 90 minutes and 4,000 TL for 2 hours. With four players that works out to roughly 500 TL per person per hour. Other clubs vary — always confirm directly before booking as prices change with the season.

Is padel cheap in Antalya compared to other Turkish cities?

Antalya is competitive with Istanbul and Ankara for padel pricing. Courts in central Istanbul run 800–1,500 TL per hour at comparable clubs. Antalya's lower operating costs and tourism season mean pricing is similar or slightly lower for premium roofed courts.

Is there a padel membership in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya offers membership packages that reduce the per-session cost for regular players. Contact the club via WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) or the Matchpoint app for current membership options — packages change seasonally.

Can I get a padel court for two people in Antalya?

Yes — courts can be booked for two players (doubles on a full court, or singles if you prefer). The court price is the same regardless of player count; fewer players means a higher cost per person.

What is included in the court hire price?

Court hire at V7 Padel Antalya includes the court and basic equipment loan (rackets and balls available if needed). Coaching, locker use and pro-shop equipment are priced separately.

How do I pay for a padel court in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya accepts payment via the Matchpoint app (card online) or at the club. Book through the app or WhatsApp (+90 546 780 0877) to reserve your slot.
