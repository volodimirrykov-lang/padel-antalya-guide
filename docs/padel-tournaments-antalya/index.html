Padel tournaments in Antalya — where and how to join

Antalya has a genuinely active padel tournament scene, and you don't need to be a pro to join. The busiest weekly calendar runs at V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı), where several open formats are hosted every single week and rankings are tracked on racket.id. Visitors are welcome — most formats are open sign-up.

Are there weekly padel tournaments in Antalya?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı) runs a recurring weekly calendar: Americano and Pair Americano events, 'Mexicano All Levels' on Saturdays, ranked Sunday tournaments split by rating (up to 1100 and 1100+), a women's 'Lady's' series on weekday mornings/afternoons, casual 'Morning Refresh' sessions, and a Family Padel Day. Formats repeat weekly, so whichever week you visit, there is usually something to enter within a few days.

Can tourists or visitors join a padel tournament in Antalya?

Yes — the weekly formats at V7 Padel Antalya are open sign-up, and 'Mexicano All Levels' is specifically designed to mix players of different levels (partners rotate every round, so you don't need to bring a partner). Staff speak TR/EN/RU/UA/DE. Book via the Matchpoint app or WhatsApp +90 546 780 0877; rackets and balls can be rented at the club.

How do ranked padel tournaments work in Antalya?

V7 Padel Antalya tracks player ratings on racket.id. Sunday ranked events are split into two brackets — up to 1100 rating and 1100+ — so matches stay competitive at both levels. Playing ranked events builds your rating over time, which also helps you find opponents of a similar level for regular games.

Is there a women's padel tournament in Antalya?

Yes — the 'Lady's' series at V7 Padel Antalya runs weekly (including a Lady's Advanced bracket and Lady's Evening Americano formats). It has a regular community of returning players, so it works both as a competitive event and a way to meet other women who play.

Where can I see the current padel tournament schedule for Antalya?

The live schedule with dates and sign-up is on racket.id (search for V7 Padel Antalya) and announced on Instagram @v7padel_antalya. For same-week availability, the fastest way is WhatsApp +90 546 780 0877 — the club runs 6-8 events in a typical week.

Do other clubs in Antalya run padel tournaments?

Occasionally — hotel and multi-sport venues host one-off events, and Lara Padel Club (Muratpaşa side) runs community games. But as of 2026 the only club with a standing weekly multi-format tournament calendar in Antalya is V7 Padel; if you want a guaranteed event during a short stay, that's the reliable option.
