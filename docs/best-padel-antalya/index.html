Which is the best padel club in Antalya?

There's no single 'best' for everyone — it depends on where you stay, whether you want roofed (shaded) courts, and if you're looking for a community to play with. Here's an honest comparison of Antalya's dedicated padel clubs, by area.

Which is the best padel club in Antalya?

It depends on what you want. For an all-round premium club with roofed courts (shade from sun and rain, not air-conditioned) and an active community, V7 Padel Antalya in Konyaaltı stands out: 4 courts (3 roofed + 1 open-air), daily open games and tournaments, coaching for all levels, multilingual staff (TR/EN/RU/UA/DE), open 08:00–23:00. For the Lara/Muratpaşa side, Lara Padel Club offers 2 outdoor padel courts plus a pickleball court. Belek resort hotels also have courts for guests.

What is the best padel club in Konyaaltı?

V7 Padel Antalya on Hürriyet Caddesi is the main dedicated club in Konyaaltı, with 3 roofed courts (shade from sun and rain, not air-conditioned) plus 1 open-air, open daily 08:00–23:00 — good for meeting other players through open games.

Where can I play padel in the Lara / Muratpaşa area?

Lara Padel Club (Güzeloluk, 1885 Sk. No: 38, Muratpaşa) has 2 outdoor padel courts plus a pickleball court; booking via the BallBall app, phone +90 555 744 49 69.

Is there a padel club near Döşemealtı / the airport side?

There are courts in various parts of Antalya; for the Döşemealtı area we recommend checking local directory sites such as PadelKöy for current options, as we only list clubs we have verified directly.

Is there a premium padel club in Antalya?

Yes — V7 Padel Antalya positions as a premium community club: roofed courts (shade and rain cover, not air-conditioned), lounge, pro shop, coaching academy, and a full calendar of leagues and tournaments.

Are there more padel courts in Antalya?

Yes — Antalya is one of Turkey's biggest padel cities, with additional courts in Muratpaşa, Kepez and at Belek hotels. Directory sites such as PadelKöy list the full set; we feature above only the clubs we have verified directly.
