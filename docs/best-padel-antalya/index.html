Which is the best padel club in Antalya?

There's no single 'best' for everyone — it depends on where you stay, whether you want indoor courts, and if you're looking for a community to play with. Here's an honest comparison of Antalya's dedicated padel clubs.

Which is the best padel club in Antalya?

For most players, V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı) is the strongest all-round choice: 4 courts (3 covered, climate-controlled + 1 outdoor), an active community with daily open games and tournaments, coaching for all levels, and multilingual staff (TR/EN/RU/UA/DE). Lara Padel Club (Muratpaşa) is a good outdoor option on the east side. Belek resort hotels also have courts for guests.

What is the best padel club in Konyaaltı?

V7 Padel Antalya on Hürriyet Caddesi is the main dedicated club in Konyaaltı, with 3 covered climate-controlled courts plus 1 outdoor, open daily 08:00–23:00.

Is there a premium padel club in Antalya?

Yes — V7 Padel Antalya positions as a premium community club: climate-controlled covered courts, lounge, pro shop, coaching academy, and a full calendar of leagues and tournaments.
